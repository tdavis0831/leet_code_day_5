About Lotus

Lotus was created by Tasha Davis, LMSW as a way to combine her previous career as a social worker, with her new career transition into engineering. During her career there were many times Tasha met with clients who did not know the signs of mental illness, how to tell if they had mental illness or where to get help. This site was created with those clients in mind as a resource for information.
The goal as Tasha continue's to work on the site is to allow users to upload images, use google maps to pin locations of providers on the same screen as the list of providers, incorporate more assessments, provide outside resources to various organizations.
